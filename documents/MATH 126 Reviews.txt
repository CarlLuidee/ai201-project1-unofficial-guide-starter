Comments on the course

Prof. Nichifor is a very nice instructor and the content of the course is pretty good as well. However, the curve is very harsh.
Advice

Your grade in this course heavily depends on how your classmates are doing. Thus, I would not recommend taking this course during autumn quarters because there will be many smart people who used AP to skip 124 and 125, this will make the curve very very bad. I got the same percentage score as my friend with the same instructor (she took it during spring), she got a 3.5 and I got a 3.0...

Comments on the course

when I took this class, most of the material was delivered through mandatory panopto videos recorded by Dr Loveless. He also had optional zoom sessions during the normal class time to go over panopto material and answer questions. (My only complaint was that it took a lot of time to watch the panopto videos and attend zoom classes, so sometimes I had to miss out and skip zoom class). Quiz section was optional but held at the time you registered for on zoom. The exams were not monitored but timed and you submitted your answers and work to webassign. He reviewed our work and gave us partial credit on the exams.
Suggest a professor

Loveless is great and he goes way out of his way to try to make online learning actually decent
