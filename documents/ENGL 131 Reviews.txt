Prof: Dan Zhu / Fall 2017

Aug 23, 2021
Comments on the course

Not very hard. Low output class with three essays that teach you foundational college writing skills, a few worksheets, and a portfolio with reflective statements about the main papers. This class was very accessible for first-year students and was a good example of how first-year writing in college SHOULD be taught.
Advice

I think Prof. Zhu was very helpful if you go in during office hours and ask for one on one help with essays. She gives really good writing advice.
Suggest a professor

My professor was very nice but she doesn't teach at UW anymore (she has a full-time job in Student Life, I think).
